## 📖 Project Overview

This project calculates personal income tax based on the new Sri Lankan tax brackets introduced in 2025. It demonstrates **functional programming concepts** including `map()`, `filter()`, `lambda` functions, and list comprehensions.

### Key Features:
- ✅ Calculate income tax based on progressive tax brackets
- ✅ Determine effective tax rates
- ✅ Calculate take-home pay (annual & monthly)
- ✅ Rank taxpayers by tax contribution
- ✅ Identify high earners (top tax bracket)
- ✅ Generate comprehensive tax reports

---

## Sri Lanka Tax Brackets (2025)

| Annual Income (LKR) | Tax Rate |
|---------------------|----------|
| Up to 1,800,000 | **0%** (Tax-free!) |
| 1,800,001 - 2,800,000 | 6% |
| 2,800,001 - 3,300,000 | 18% |
| 3,300,001 - 3,800,000 | 24% |
| 3,800,001 - 4,300,000 | 30% |
| Greater than 4,300,000 | 36% |
